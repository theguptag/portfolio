Cooby- Supercharge WhatsApp for Work

April 26, 2022. 3min read

We use WhatsApp on a regular basis, and it has become a part of our daily routine.

It becomes difficult for us to manage all of the chats we receive. Here comes Cooby- Supercharge WhatsApp for Work , a chrome extension that will greatly assist us in sorting and categorising the messages.

After installing the extension, categories such as Unread, Awaiting reply, Needs reply, Groups and more will appear on the top of the bar.

There is also a customization option, such as a work category, which allows you to customise or add people who are related to your work field.



I highly recommend to use this extension and give it a try!
